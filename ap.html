English AP Quiz for Non-Native English Learners

1. What is the correct form of the verb in this sentence: "She _____ to the store every day."



20. What is the antonym of “ancient”?

Modern
Old
Historic

Why? "Modern" means recent or current, which is the opposite of "ancient."

21. Choose the correct sentence:

She doesn’t like apples.
She don’t like apples.
She didn’t likes apples.

Why? Use "doesn’t" for third person singular.

22. “He ____ to the store yesterday.” Choose the correct verb.

went
goes
gone

Why? "Went" is the past tense of "go."

23. What does “bilingual” mean?

Speaking two languages
Speaking only one language
Speaking many languages

Why? “Bi” means two, “lingual” means language.

24. Which word is an adjective?

Beautiful
Quickly
Run

Why? "Beautiful" describes a noun.

25. What is the plural form of “child”?

Children
Childs
Childes

Why? "Children" is the irregular plural form of "child."

26. Complete the sentence: “I have lived in Beijing ____ five years.”

for
since
in

Why? Use “for” with a length of time.

27. “I’m looking forward to ____ you.”

meeting
meet
met

Why? “Looking forward to” is followed by a gerund (-ing form).

28. Which sentence is correct?

There are some books on the table.
There is some books on the table.
There be books on the table.

Why? Use "are" for plural nouns like “books.”

29. “Could you help me, ____?”

please
now
fast

Why? "Please" makes a polite request.

30. Which word is a preposition?

under
run
quick

Why? "Under" shows position or location.

31. Choose the correct past tense verb:

saw
see
seeing

Why? "Saw" is the past of "see."

32. What is the opposite of “expensive”?

cheap
large
long

Why? “Cheap” means low cost.

33. Which is an uncountable noun?

water
bottles
chairs

Why? You can’t count water without a container.

34. “How ____ apples do you want?”

many
much
more

Why? Use “many” with countable nouns like apples.

35. Choose the correct comparison:

Faster than
Fastest than
More fast

Why? "Faster than" is the correct comparative form.

36. What does “career” mean?

A long-term job path
A holiday
A game

Why? A career is the work you do over time.

37. Choose the correct article: “____ elephant is big.”

An
A
The

Why? Use “an” before vowel sounds.

38. “Don’t forget to bring your umbrella, ____ it rains.”

in case
so that
although

Why? "In case" prepares for a possibility.

39. Which is a conjunction?

because
quickly
money

Why? Conjunctions connect ideas — like "because."

40. What is the correct past participle of “eat”?

eaten
ate
eated

Why? "Eaten" is used with “have” in perfect tenses.
